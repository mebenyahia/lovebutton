Love Button

About


Valentine's Day is often associated with love, but for many people it can also be a time of loneliness. You may not have anyone to tell you nice things but I've got you! Love can mean many things: it can be the love you have for your friends, your family, your special one and even the love you have for yourself.
Exactly one year ago I thought about how cool it'd be to have a love button that one can click or tap to hear something nice, and now this year, when February 2023 rolled around and I needed a pick me up; I came across a scratch file with this idea on it and decided to work on it on a whim. I had a blast creating it and experimenting with different prompts. It's probably one of the cringiest or funniest things I ever created, but I hope that this little wonky app with a very uncanny valley voice for each prompt reminds you to love yourself, to love others, and that you matter.
--With lots of love and a pinch of hope that this makes your day!
-Meryem
